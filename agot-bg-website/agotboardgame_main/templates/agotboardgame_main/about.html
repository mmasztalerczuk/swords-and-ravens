What is Swords and Ravens?

Swords and Ravens is a online adaptation of the excellent board game A Game of Thrones: The Board Game (Second Edition) edited by Fantasy Flight Games. You can play with players from around the world either in live or via email, for free.

It is developed mainly by Longwelwind, with the considerable help of:

  • Gereon and Flutterbit for their contributions to the codebase.
  • Tex for hosting communication about the game on the AGOT:BG Discord.
  • OrangePeanut, Marco Martell, DerSeynmaster, obmit, DemonInside, Jason77, Master Falcao and others for play-testing the game during the open beta-test.
